==================

==================
Nested Links Syntax (not valid in MediaWiki)
==================

[[Outer Link|[Inner Link]]]

---

(document
  (newline)
  (wikilink
    (target)
    (label
      (external_link
        (target)
        (label
          (text)))))
  (newline))
